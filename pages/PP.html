Privacy Policy

How we protect your information and data

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At Global Money Lens, your privacy is our top priority. We are committed to protecting your personal information and being transparent about how we use it. This policy explains, in simple terms, what data we collect and how we keep it safe.

Information We Collect

  • Email address: Collected when you subscribe to our newsletter or updates.
  • Usage data: We collect anonymous analytics data, such as page visits and time spent on our site, to improve your experience.
  • Contact form submissions: If you contact us, we collect the information you provide (such as your name and email).

How We Use Your Data

  • To send you financial updates, blog content, and newsletters you’ve subscribed to.
  • To improve our website and provide a better user experience.
  • To manage your subscriptions and respond to your inquiries.

Cookies

We use cookies to understand how visitors use our website and to improve performance. Cookies help us analyze traffic and personalize your experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

Data Protection

  • Your data is stored securely using industry-standard security practices.
  • We never sell your personal information to third parties.
  • Access to your data is limited to authorized team members only.

Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services, such as analytics tools or email providers, to help us operate our website. These services may collect data according to their own privacy policies.

Your Rights

  • You can unsubscribe from our emails at any time using the link in each message.
  • You can request to view, update, or delete your personal data by contacting us.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised date.
