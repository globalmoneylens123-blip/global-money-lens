About Thashmina Sandeepa

I am the founder of Global Money Lens, a modern finance platform dedicated to delivering clear, strategic, and actionable financial insights for a global audience.

With a focus on investing, wealth-building, and financial intelligence, my mission is to simplify complex financial concepts into practical knowledge that individuals can apply in real life to build long-term financial independence.

Every article is designed with precision — focusing on real-world strategies in investing, saving, and income generation, rather than theory alone.

This is not just a blog. It is a curated financial intelligence platform built for individuals who are serious about improving their financial future.
